ELT Risk Heatmap

One-time setup — adds a bookmark to your browser bar so you can load the live heatmap on the SmartSheet dashboard with a single click.

1
Make sure your bookmarks bar is visible in Chrome: press Ctrl+Shift+B to toggle it on.
2
Drag the button below up to your bookmarks bar and drop it there. It will appear as a bookmark called "ELT Heatmap".
3
You're done. Open the SmartSheet ELT dashboard, then click that bookmark once — the live heatmap will replace the video widget instantly.
